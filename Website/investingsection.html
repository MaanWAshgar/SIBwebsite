Investing is spending your savings to buy assets that are expected to return more value as time goes on. There are multiple different ways to invest and each carry with it certain risks and offer different level of rewards. Some investing method requires your time and money while others will let your money work by itself to generate more money or a steady income that can help you achieve your goals.
